Upper Palaeolithic Revolution

~50,000 BC (+70,000 years)

  • Described as "Great leap forward [1]" for human kind.
  • Humans burying their dead, making clothes with animal hide, hunting with trapping pits, engaging in cave paintings
  • Still largely hunter-gatherers
[1] Diamond, Jared (1999). Guns, Germs, and Steel: The Fates of Human Societies. New York

Birth of Agriculture

~10,000 BC (+40,000 years)

  • Early domestication of plants
  • First farming settlements around Meditierranean Sea to Iran[1].
[1] Balter, Michael (2013), Farming Was So Nice, It Was Invented At Least Twice.

Chalcolithic (Early Bronze Age)

~5,000 BC (+5,000 years)
